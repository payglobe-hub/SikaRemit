# 🏪 SikaRemit Merchant App

## 🎯 **Purpose**
Complete business management and Soft POS terminal application for merchants.

## 🚀 **Key Features**
- **📱 Soft POS Terminal**: Transform smartphone into payment terminal
- **💳 NFC Payments**: Accept contactless card payments
- **📱 Mobile Money**: Process MTN, Telecel, AirtelTigo, Glo payments
- **📊 Analytics Dashboard**: Sales insights, revenue tracking, growth analytics
- **📱 Device Management**: Register and monitor POS devices
- **🧾 Receipt Management**: Digital receipts, email/SMS sharing, printing
- **📈 Business Analytics**: Real-time metrics, customer insights, payment trends
- **🔐 Security Features**: PCI DSS compliant security, device monitoring
- **⚙️ Business Settings**: Merchant profile, configuration management
